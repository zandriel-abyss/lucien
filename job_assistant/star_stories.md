# STAR Stories (Seed)

Add your reusable STAR stories here.

## Example Story
- **Situation:** A payments onboarding flow had high drop-off for SMEs.
- **Task:** Increase completion while meeting compliance requirements.
- **Action:** Led product, design, data science, and compliance to redesign KYC flow and launch staged verification.
- **Result:** Improved completion and reduced manual review backlog.
